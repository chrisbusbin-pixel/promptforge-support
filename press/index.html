Common Press Questions

Q: Why a portfolio instead of one big product?
Different audiences need different products. Trying to merge them into one umbrella brand dilutes everything. Each app gets its own positioning, its own voice, its own marketing.

Q: What's the through-line?
One-time pricing wherever possible, on-device storage by default, no ads, no vendor marketplace games. The apps are about doing one specific job well, not capturing your data and reselling it.

Q: Who builds them?
Indie operation. Solo developer with occasional help on specific projects.
