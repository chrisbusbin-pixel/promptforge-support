Why we built it

Cozi answers "where do we need to be." It does that well. But the question many parents are actually asking is: "how do I get my 8-year-old to consistently do their part without me asking three times?"

That's not a calendar problem. That's a motivation, structure, and accountability problem. Streaks work. Visible progress works. Earned rewards work. Kids don't need a Google-Calendar-with-lists. They need a chart they can interact with, see their progress on, and feel proud of.

What Kid Mode actually does

  • Simplified UI appropriate for ages 4–10. No settings to break, no in-app purchases, no notifications they shouldn't see.
  • Today's chores as the only screen. Tap, complete, see the streak grow.
  • No data collection. Kids never enter personal info. No accounts.
  • Parent-locked settings with a PIN. Kids can't accidentally change their list or skip a streak.
