Why "free" wedding apps aren't free

The Knot and WeddingWire are owned by the same parent company. Their core business is selling vendor visibility — caterers, florists, photographers, venues, DJs, planners pay to reach engaged couples. When you sign up:

  • Your contact info becomes part of a marketplace where vendors who pay get visibility on your profile.
  • You start getting emails. Calls. Texts. Some helpful, most not.
  • Your wedding date and budget become a sales lead.

It's not a moral failing — it's just the business model. They built it that way because vendors will pay for access to engaged couples ready to spend $30k on average.

Some couples want that — they actively want vendor outreach. For them, The Knot is fine.

If you don't want that, you have a different need. That's what WeddingDay solves.
