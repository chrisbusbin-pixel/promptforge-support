Frequently Asked Questions

Does DebtFree connect to my bank?No. You enter balances manually. No bank login, no account numbers, no third-party financial-data middlemen. Your financial information never leaves your device.
What's the difference between snowball and avalanche?Snowball method pays off smallest balances first — quick wins build momentum. Avalanche method pays off highest interest rates first — saves the most money mathematically. DebtFree supports both. Switch anytime.
How is this different from YNAB or EveryDollar?YNAB and EveryDollar are full budget management apps that require connecting your bank. DebtFree is a focused debt-payoff planner — no bank link, no subscription, just $3.99 one-time. Use it alongside any budget app you already trust, or use it standalone.
What kinds of debt can I track?Credit cards, student loans, medical debt, auto loans, personal loans, mortgages, family loans — any debt with a balance, interest rate, and minimum payment.
What does the what-if tool do?Simulate extra payments, interest-rate changes, or balance changes — see exactly how each move shifts your payoff date and total interest paid. Helps you find where every extra dollar has the biggest impact.
Will there be an Android or web version?iOS only at this time. Email chrisbusbin@gmail.com if you'd like other platforms.
