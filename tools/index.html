About these tools

All tools and references are built and maintained by Chris Busbin, founder of a portfolio of indie iOS apps. The tools complement the apps — for example, the Credit Card Payoff Calculator handles one debt; the DebtFree iOS app handles your full debt payoff plan.

Calculators run entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server. Refresh the page and your inputs are gone.
