Frequently Asked Questions

What is PromptForge?A native iOS app for power users of ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other LLMs. Save your best prompts, organize them in folders, use {{variable}} templates, and track your AI subscription spend.
How much does PromptForge cost?$4.99 USD as a one-time purchase. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no ads.
Does PromptForge work offline?Yes. All prompts are stored locally on your device. No internet required.
Is my data private?Absolutely. Zero data collection. All prompts and settings stored locally on-device. No servers, no accounts, no analytics.
How is this different from AIPRM?AIPRM is a Chrome extension that requires an account and a subscription. PromptForge is a native iOS app, $4.99 one-time, no account, no subscription, and all data stays on your device.
Will there be an Android or web version?Currently iOS-only. Email chrisbusbin@gmail.com if you'd like other platforms.
Does it support image-gen prompts (Midjourney, Flux, etc.)?Yes. Prompts are AI-agnostic. Save Midjourney parameter prompts, Flux templates, ChatGPT prompts, Claude prompts — all in one library.
