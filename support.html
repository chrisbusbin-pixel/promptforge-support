Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a prompt?Tap the + button in the Library tab, choose "Write from Scratch" or "Use Prompt Builder," then fill in your prompt details and tap Save.
How do I organize prompts into folders?Go to the Folders tab to create folders with custom names and emoji icons. You can move prompts between folders from the prompt detail screen.
How do I find new prompts?Visit the Discover tab to browse 200+ curated prompt templates across 11 categories. Tap "Add to Library" to save any template.
Can I export my prompts?Yes! Go to Settings and tap "Export Prompts" to save your entire library as a JSON file you can share or back up.
Can I import prompts?Yes. Go to Settings and tap "Import Prompts" to load prompts from a JSON file.
Is my data private?Absolutely. All your prompts are stored locally on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking. Your data never leaves your phone.
Will I get new prompt templates?Yes! We deliver new templates through over-the-air updates, so you get fresh content without waiting for an App Store update.
