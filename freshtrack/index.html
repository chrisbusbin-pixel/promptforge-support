The food-waste problem (and what FreshTrack does about it)

The USDA estimates households waste roughly 30% of the food they buy — about $1,500 per year for a family of four. Most of that waste happens because items get pushed to the back of the fridge, forgotten in the freezer, or buried in the pantry.

FreshTrack solves it with three simple features.

What FreshTrack does

Inventory in secondsAdd an item with a name, location (fridge / freezer / pantry), and expiration. Done.
Expiration remindersGet notified before food goes bad — daily summary or custom alerts. Use it up before it gets thrown out.
Family rhythmBuilt for households where multiple people shop and cook. Update inventory once, everyone sees what's there.
Privacy-firstNo accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your inventory stays on your device.

FAQ

Does it scan barcodes?FreshTrack supports quick item entry. Check the App Store listing for current scanning features in this version.
Does it work for freezer items?Yes. Fridge, freezer, and pantry are tracked separately so freezer items stay visible.
How does it differ from a Notes app?FreshTrack tracks expiration and pushes you reminders. Notes apps don't surface expiring items.
Will there be Android or web?iOS only currently.
